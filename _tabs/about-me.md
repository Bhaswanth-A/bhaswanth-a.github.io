<span style="font-size:1.5em;">Hey there! I am Bhaswanth Ayapilla.</span>

I recently completed my Master's in Robotic Systems Development (MRSD) program at Carnegie Mellon University. I am joining Path Robotics as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I will be focusing on robot learning.

During my Master's program, I completed my capstone project in space robotics — developing the Lunar ROADSTER, a Robotic Operator for Autonomous Development of Surface Trails and Exploration Routes on the Moon. This exciting project was conducted under the supervision of [Dr. William "Red" Whittaker](https://www.ri.cmu.edu/ri-faculty/william-red-l-whittaker/). You can learn more about it here: [Lunar ROADSTER](https://bhaswanth-a.github.io/posts/lunar-roadster-cmu/).

I also conducted research in CMU’s [DRIVE Lab](https://drive-lab-cmu.github.io/), under the supervision of [Dr. John Dolan](https://www.ri.cmu.edu/ri-faculty/john-m-dolan/), and the [BIG Lab](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~jeanoh/big/), under the supervision of [Dr. Jean Oh](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~jeanoh/). As part of my work in the BIG Lab, I collaborated with Lavoro AI and Bosch AI, working on humanoids, manipulators, and VLAs. Prior to this, I completed a summer internship at Milwaukee Tool as a Robotics Engineer, developing a mobile robot integrated with a robotic manipulator.
